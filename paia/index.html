2. Information officer

Requests under PAIA and queries regarding personal information should be directed to the Information Officer at the contact details above (or as updated on this website).

3. Guide of the Information Regulator

Members of the public may obtain a copy of the Guide on how to exercise rights under PAIA and related legislation from the Information Regulator of South Africa.

4. Categories of records

Trust Co holds records in categories including, where applicable, client and matter files, correspondence, financial and billing records, human resources records, conflict checks, and regulatory compliance records. Not all records are automatically accessible; exemptions may apply.

5. Request procedure

Access requests must be made in writing using the prescribed form where required, specifying the records sought and the requester’s identity. We will respond within the statutory period and may charge a reasonable fee as permitted by law.

6. Grounds for refusal

Access may be refused where PAIA or POPIA permits or requires refusal, including legal professional privilege, confidentiality, third-party privacy, or ongoing commercial or legal prejudice.

7. Remedies

If you are dissatisfied with our decision, you may apply to court or follow the procedures prescribed by the Information Regulator.

8. Availability

This manual is available on our website. A PDF copy may be provided on request for record-keeping purposes. Replace this sentence if your firm maintains a separate PDF version.
