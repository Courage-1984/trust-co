# Trust Co (Pty) Ltd — Business overview

**What:** Gauteng-based legal firm focused on **insolvency, restructuring, and trust law** — combining technical legal work with commercial judgment.

**Who we serve:** Individuals, companies, directors, creditors, investors, and other stakeholders in distressed or fiduciary situations.

**Jurisdiction:** South African law, including **Companies Act** frameworks; **area served:** South Africa (base **Gauteng**).

**Website:** [trustco.co.za](https://trustco.co.za/)

## Core services

| Service | Focus |
|--------|--------|
