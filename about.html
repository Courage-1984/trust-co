Our Identity

Legal Expertise. Commercial Understanding.

Trust Co (Pty) Ltd is a Gauteng-based legal firm specialising in insolvency, restructuring, and trust law. Our team combines legal expertise with commercial understanding, ensuring that every matter is approached with both technical excellence and practical insight.

We work alongside individuals, companies, directors, creditors, and stakeholders — providing clarity and decisive legal action at every critical juncture. Our mandate is not simply to process legal matters, but to achieve the best possible outcomes for those we serve.
